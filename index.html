The moat

Knowledge Base MCP — Every build feeds structured learnings back into the system. What worked, what failed, what signals mattered, what was noise. After 50 builds, this isn't a notes folder. It's a compound asset that makes decision-making systematically better.

Human non-negotiables

AI orchestrates. Humans decide. These checkpoints never get automated:

◆ Go/no-go — Whether to build at all
◆ Scope lock — What's in, what's out
◆ Design approval — Visual/UX sign-off
◆ Ship decision — Ready or not ready
◆ Kill/continue — Post-launch verdict
