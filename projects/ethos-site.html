This site. Public-facing working notebook documenting the solo AI product incubator — methodology, project log, builds.

Purpose

Not a marketing site. A working document that happens to be public. Tracks what's being built, what was learned, and what changed along the way. The site itself is one of the incubator's products — iterated on like anything else.

Design Decisions

Static HTML + CSS, no framework. IBM Plex Mono + Source Serif 4. Dark theme with warm accent (#d4a574). Minimal formatting — content-first, no decorative elements. Responsive down to mobile.

Content distributed between this site (curated public narrative, paragraphs) and Notion (full internal record, pages). Website shows what matters; Notion tracks everything.

Structure

  • / — Methodology loop, current focus, operating principles
  • /projects — All projects with status and detail pages
  • /log — Running record of decisions and builds

Status

On hold as an active development focus — updated as a byproduct of other work. Each project build and decision gets logged here, but the site itself isn't being actively redesigned.
